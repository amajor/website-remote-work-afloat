# Welcome to the Remote Work Afloat & Loop Life Academy Community

_Welcome to the Remote Work Afloat and Loop Life Academy community! Both this site and Loop Life Academy are Tea & Tides Stories projects._

Whether you’re exploring the Great Loop or living a life afloat, this space is for cruisers, remote workers, and adventurous families. We’re here to share stories, tips, and resources to help you live your best cruising life—no matter where you are!
