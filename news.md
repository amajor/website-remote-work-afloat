## New Book Remote Work Afloat Helps Aspiring Loopers & Cruisers Work from the Water

Remote Work Afloat is now available! This essential guide by experienced liveaboard and Gold Looper Alison Major helps aspiring Loopers and cruisers navigate the challenges of working remotely from a boat. Learn how to stay connected, manage power, and create a productive workspace while balancing work and life on the water.
